Our Mission

Records that stay with you after the last note.

Welcome to Seraphim Records, a record store for people who like to slow down, drop the needle and listen on purpose.

The store brings together albums that feel good to own and to play. From the spacey pull of The Dark Side of the Moon to the bright confidence of Abbey Road and the washed colours of Currents, every title is chosen because it leaves something behind after the music stops.

Sometimes that is a line like all you touch and all you see, sometimes it is the quiet feeling you get when the last track fades. Either way, our aim is simple: help you leave with music that actually fits your taste and your space.

What We Provide

  • Vintage and modern vinyl curated for sound and feel
  • Clear details on pressings, editions and reissues
  • Limited and hard to find records for collectors
  • Suggestions based on artists, moods or favourite eras
  • Pre orders and special requests where possible

The Music That Shapes Us

Built around albums that mean something.

The catalogue behind Seraphim Records leans on albums that still get talked about long after release. Records like Grace, Yeezus, Abbey Road and Currents are not just popular, they are reference points. They are the records people play when they want to feel something specific.

We treat every new addition through that lens. Does it hold up next to the giants, or at least stand confidently in its own lane? If the answer is yes, it earns a spot in the collection.
