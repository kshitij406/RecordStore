ABOUT US

Seraphim Records is your go-to destination for vinyl enthusiasts and music lovers alike. Established in 1995, we have been curating an extensive collection of records spanning various genres, from classic rock to contemporary indie. Our passion for music drives us to provide a unique shopping experience, where customers can explore rare finds, new releases, and timeless classics. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your vinyl journey, Seraphim Records is here to help you discover the perfect soundtrack for your life.
